Clibano S

Clibano S comes with voice control and QR / barcode scanner. Using new technology, the scanner will send the required information to the oven and prepare the meal in the best possible way. The function is designed to make cooking easier for the benefit of the elderly. Clibano S has the option of manually adjusting heat and time, if desired. The oven can be controlled by voice and can respond with voice, as well as having internet connection. The voice function is made to help elderly with impaired vision. The door has an integrated display showing the necessary information about the meal. The large screen serves as the oven display and is enlarged to satisfy older people's use. The door shows for example, temperature, time and program. The technology that Clibano S offers helps you get the information you want, either visually or by audio.
